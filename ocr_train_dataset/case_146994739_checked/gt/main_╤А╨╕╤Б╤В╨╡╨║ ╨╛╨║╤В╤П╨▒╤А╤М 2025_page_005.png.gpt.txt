6. Required documents:  
- Original and 4 (four) copies of the commercial invoice for the supply of goods. The invoice indicates  
the name of the goods, the quantity of goods, the price, cost, country of origin of the goods and the  
customs code of the goods for which customs clearance is carried out.  
- Proforma invoice in 5 copies.  
- Original and 4 (four) copies of the packing list for each item of packaging (pallet). The packing list  
indicates the net weight of 1 pc. goods, total net weight, gross weight, quantity of goods.  
- Certificate of origin of goods, indicating each position of the supplied goods.  
All documents sent electronically are valid until they are completely replaced with documents with the  
original seal.  
7. Packing:  
The packaging of the goods must protect the goods from moisture, rust, erosion and impact, and  
must be suitable for sea/intermodal transport. The seller is responsible for any damage to the goods or  
their loss due to poor packaging. Each item of packaging must be labeled with dimensions, gross weight,  
net weight and indelible ink warning labels such as "Do not turn over", "Protect from moisture", "Handle  
with care" and "Center of gravity".  
8. Shipping order:  
After the shipment of the goods, but no later than 7 days later, the SELLER, by any means available to  
him, sends to the BUYER the original commercial documents for the shipped consignment of goods,  
necessary for customs clearance in the country of the importer:  
- commercial invoice in 5 copies.  
- Proforma invoice in 5 copies.  
- packing list in 5 copies.  
9. Liability of the parties:  
9.1. The delivery of the Goods is not made within the terms agreed by the parties, the SELLER pays the
BUYER a fine calculated at the rate of 1.0% of the vlue of the goods not delivered on time for each day 
of delay.
9.2. Payments of the fine does not release the Seller from the obligation to fulfill the contract.  
9.3. Increase of delivery goods that do not meet the terms of the contract in terms of quality,  
The SELLER pays the BUYER a penalty in the amount of 100.0% of the original  
the value of the rejected goods.  
9.4. Payment of a fine for breach of contract does not release the seller from compensation for damages  
caused to the Buyer due to the seller's failure to comply with the terms of the contract, and from the  
obligation to fulfill the contract.  
9.5. In case of discrepancy between the quantity, quality, ordered goods, the Buyer has the right to  
demand payment of a fine in the amount of 100% of the value of the undelivered Goods.  
9.6. If 50% or more of the goods do not meet the declared quality, the Buyer has the right to demand  
payment of a fine in the amount of 100% of the contract value. In this case, the Buyer returns the entire  
consignment of goods to the Seller, all costs for the return of the goods are borne by the Seller.  
10. Disputes:  
All disputes and disagreements that may arise in connection with the fulfillment of obligations under this  
contract shall be resolved by the parties through negotiations. In the event that disputes are not resolved  
through negotiations, the dispute is referred to the Arbitration Court of Kazakhstan for resolution.  
The applicable law under this agreement is the law of Kazakhstan.  
The decisions of the arbitration will be final and binding on the Parties.
